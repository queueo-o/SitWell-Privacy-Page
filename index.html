Privacy Policy

Overview

SitWell is a posture-monitoring app that works with a physical hardware sensor pad. This policy explains what data the app handles, where it is stored, and what your rights are. The short version: your data stays on your device. We don't collect it, send it anywhere, or share it with anyone.

No Account Required

SitWell does not ask you to create an account or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or date of birth. You can use the app fully without identifying yourself to us in any way.

What Data the App Uses

SitWell reads pressure sensor data from the hardware pad over a local Bluetooth connection to calculate your real-time posture score. Your session history and posture trends — such as scores over time and slouch counts — are stored locally on the hardware microcontroller itself. This information never leaves your device, is not uploaded to any server, and is not accessible to us.

Your posture data is stored on your hardware pad only. It is not sent to any server, cloud service, or third party — ever.

Permissions We Do Not Use

SitWell does not request access to your camera, microphone, or location at any time. No such permissions are required to use the app.

No Third-Party Services

SitWell does not integrate with any third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, crash-reporting services, or cloud providers. There is no code in the app that transmits your data to any external party.

No User-Submitted Content

SitWell does not allow you to upload files, images, or any other content. Nothing you do in the app is transmitted or stored outside your device.

How to Delete Your Data

You can permanently erase all stored session history and posture data at any time. Open the app, go to Settings, and tap the Clear Data button. This action is immediate and cannot be undone.

Children's Privacy

SitWell is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. Since the app collects no personal data at all, no special procedures for children are required.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is ever updated, the new version will be made available through the App Store listing or within the app itself. We encourage you to review it periodically. Continued use of the app after any change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

SitWell is a personal wellness tool designed to help you become more aware of your sitting habits. It is not a medical device, and it is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition, injury, or health problem.

Accuracy of Posture Scores

The posture scores and feedback provided by SitWell are estimates based on pressure readings from the sensor pad. They are intended as general guidance only. Sensor readings can be affected by factors such as pad placement, chair type, clothing, body weight distribution, and movement. Results may not reflect your actual posture with clinical precision.

If you have a pre-existing back condition, pain, injury, or any health concern related to your posture or musculoskeletal system, please consult a qualified healthcare professional before relying on this app for guidance.
